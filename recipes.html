Sweet and Fruity Salad Recipes

Assorted Mix take-away



This great mixture of summer fruits can serve as a salad and desert It is the ideal on the go choice when in a hurry.

Strawberry and Kiwi mix



A cranberry, Strawberry and kiwi mix in plant-based soja yoghurt.

Exotic Fruits Mix



The classic exotic touch! Pineapple slices, papaya and other exotic fruits mixed with a bit of lime.

Crunchy and Nutty Salad Recipes

Coconut and Cashew Mix



Coconut slices with fried Cashew nuts and lukewarm strawberries. The strawberries are heated just enough to let the juice out.

Peach and Muesli mix



This is a real quick fix for the real hasty type. This can be served as a salad or for breakfast.

Nuts in Yoghurt mix



This is the ideal salad for real nut lovers. A mixture of nuts soaked in cool soya plant yoghurt. No sugar is needed. The sweetness from the raisins do the job

Hot and Spicy Salad Recipes

Spicy veg on Toast Mix Mix



If you love spicy food, then try this! Pretty easy to make. Cucumber, tomatoes and thin slices of beetroot on toast with a touch of black pepper.

Rocket Salad mix



Rocket salad with tomatoes and black pepper. The spicy flavour of the pepper dulls the bitterness of the the rocket salad leaves.

Avocado Black Pepper mix



One of our favorites. Salty tomatoes and ripe avocado slices with sweet corn. The whole mixture is put in a wrap with a touch of blackpepper. Sumptuous!
